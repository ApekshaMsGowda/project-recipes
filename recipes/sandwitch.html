Halloumi, Arugula and Tomato Sandwiches

Here's a simple recipe for a delicious Halloumi, Arugula and Tomato Sandwich!

Description

This Halloumi Sandwich combines crispy, golden halloumi cheese with fresh arugula, juicy tomatoes, and toasted bread for a delicious blend of flavors and textures. The salty richness of the cheese pairs perfectly with the freshness of the vegetables, creating a balanced and satisfying bite.

Quick and easy to prepare, this sandwich is perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a light dinner. Serve it warm with a side of fries, salad, or your favorite dipping sauce for a wholesome and flavorful meal that's ready in just minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of your favorite bread
  • 8 oz halloumi cheese, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh arugula
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil.
  2. Place the halloumi slices in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
  3. While the halloumi is cooking, toast the bread slices to your desired level of crispiness.
  4. Once the halloumi is cooked, remove it from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Assemble the sandwiches by placing arugula and tomato slices on one slice of bread, then top with the cooked halloumi slices. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Top with the second slice of bread, cut in half if desired, and serve immediately.
